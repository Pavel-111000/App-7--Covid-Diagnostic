Covid-19 Diagnostic Questionnaire

Are you afraid you might have Covid-19, but wonder if it is simply a cold, the flu, or seasonal allergies? Answer a few short questions to find out!

One thing to note concerning symptoms: Covid-19 symptoms last 7-25 days, Cold symptoms last less than 14 days, Flu symptoms last 7-14 days, and Seasonal Allergies can last for several weeks.
